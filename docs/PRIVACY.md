# Privacy

HADocs follows **Local First** and **Privacy First** principles.

- Analysis runs locally.
- No telemetry or analytics are collected.
- No Home Assistant data is uploaded automatically.
- No external AI services are called.
- Knowledge Packs and exports remain local until you choose to share them.

Generated reports may contain sensitive installation details. Review them before sharing and use the redacted Knowledge Pack where appropriate.

For credential handling and vulnerability reporting, see [SECURITY.md](../SECURITY.md).

See also the [Knowledge Pack](guides/features/Knowledge-Pack.md), or return to the [documentation home](README.md).
